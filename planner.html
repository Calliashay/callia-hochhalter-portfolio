Technical Pivot

Trimming 120-page "bloat" to a sleek, manual 75-page hyperlinked system.

Results

12 Total Sales, with a 5x increase following the technical overhaul.

Strategic Evolution

This project was a bridge between design and marketing analytics. What began as a simple weekly layout evolved into a complex, fully hyperlinked product through 53 iterations and over 50 hours of manual labor.

2 Sales (Initial Layout)
10 Sales (Hyperlinked Version)

The data validated the decision to invest 40+ additional hours into the technical overhaul.

Marketing & Conversion Optimization

  • Foundations & Trust: Created iPad mockups to establish the visual credibility required for stranger-market purchases.
  • SEO Pivot: Swapped keyword-stuffed titles for a clean "2026 Hyperlinked Digital Planner," resulting in an organic sale within 48 hours.
  • Price Strategy: Dropped pricing from $2.99 to $0.99 during finals season to remove final barriers to purchase.

Lessons from the Trenches

Research over Rework: Trusting AI advice for software limitations (Canva) led to significant technical debt; choosing the right "tech stack" is as critical as the data itself.
The "Stranger" Market: Realized that marketing is a living response to human reaction; small changes in titles and hooks drive psychological engagement.
Resilience: Balancing 30-40 work hours weekly with this project proved that professional-grade functionality is non-negotiable—"it almost works" isn't good enough.
