Stack

RStudio Excel

Scale

Company

Enterprise Mobility (Internship Project)

Project Overview

This study was conducted during my internship at Enterprise Mobility to determine the ROI of investing in automatic car wash systems for busy locations. It served as a foundational experience in gathering raw field data, managing a dataset of 300+ points in Excel, and running statistical tests in RStudio to present findings to corporate stakeholders.

Technical Methodology

Data Stewardship: Utilized Excel to maintain a clean, organized record of over 300 individual data points, ensuring integrity throughout the collection phase.
Statistical Rigor: Leveraged RStudio to perform the final analysis, looking for statistical significance between manual labor and automated system performance.
Predictable Performance: The study confirmed that the SensaWash system provided a highly predictable outcome, cleaning vehicles in a consistent 4-minute window.

Critical Reflection

A major takeaway from this project was the importance of isolating variables in data collection. While the initial results showed parity between systems, a retrospective audit revealed a need for a tighter focus on exterior-only cleaning times to eliminate the noise of interior detailing data.

  • Methodology: Improved my ability to define KPIs that align strictly with stakeholder objectives.
  • Growth: This project shaped my current analytical standard, emphasizing the need for 100% confidence in the data stack before moving to the recommendation phase.
